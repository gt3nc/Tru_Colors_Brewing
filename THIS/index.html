The Movement

What is Tru Colors?

In June of 2017, 11 gang leaders made an agreement to keep the streets safe in Wilmington with a no-shots-fired truce. In exchange for the leaders’ commitment to suppress gun violence, George Taylor told the guys he would teach them business and launch a business they would all have ownership stakes in.

The TRU Colors’ team now has the opportunity to operate a brewery that will be equipped with a 30 barrel system and will distribute all over North Carolina. The beer will be brewed by one of the best brewmasters in the southeast and will feature unique creations, from the Northside Henny IPA to the Southside Stout.

The brewpub in Wilmington, NC will be full of provocative gang insignia and history and will be a place for everyone in the community to come together and converse truthfully over delicious food and an ice cold beer.

Impact

Impact on Gang Violence

The co-owners of TRU Colors Brewing have displayed what it means to be self-reliant and have gotten back to the original values and principles that were established during the early formations of gangs.

TRU Colors Brewing Company is redefining gang culture using business and beer to create peace and unity in the inner city of Wilmington. With the guys holding themselves accountable for the state of the community, they have been successful in suppressing gun violence. TRU Colors has created an unprecedented business model that has sparked gang leadership to form allegiances and work hand-in-hand to uplift one another and take responsibility for their own communities.

The goal of the TRU Colors Brewing isn't just to create great beer. TRU Colors will be a lifestyle. As they begin to scale, TRU Colors Brewing Company plans to hire more than 100 active gang members over the first 18 months of operation to help provide opportunity and increase economic growth in local communities.
