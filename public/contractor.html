Want to hire a TRU Colors Apprentice?

Our brewery is a big project and takes some time to get going! In the meantime, we are recruiting and vetting the rest of our team. TCB will provide opportunity to gang members who want to work.

To make this happen, we’ve setup the TCB Apprentice Program. Here’s the deal: Anyone who wants a position in the brewery must first successfully complete 2 months in the TCB Apprentice Program (it could be longer if a TCB position is not immediately available).

If you’re interested in learning more about hiring a TCB Apprentice (and they really are awesome), sign up below.

TCB Apprentices make great employees. They’re thoroughly vetted (see below) and ready to work hard — after all, their success with you is required for them to get their dream career at TCB.

  • Initial skills and background questionnaire

  • Psychometric Assessment

  • Interview with Insight Profiles (www.insightprofiling.com)

  • Final peer review by TRU Colors founding members (requires unanimous vote)

  • One week at the gym to get into shape before they work for you
