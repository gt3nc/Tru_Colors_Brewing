What is TRU Colors?

TRU Colors Brewing is not your average brewery. Sure, we make great beer, but being TRU is a way of life. We represent taking the path less traveled. We represent opportunity. We represent unity. We plan to show this in our beers, our restaurants, and most importantly, our actions.

In June of 2017, 11 gang leaders came together to form TRU Colors Brewing and put a stop to gang violence in Wilmington. Active Bloods, Crips, and Growth & Development (formerly Gangster Disciples) make up the venture. We are all striving to shift gang culture into a positive influence in our communities as well as inspiring others to end the violence.

*TCB staff must remain affiliated to keep influencing other members*

The result? Not one (zero, zilch, none) shot fired by any of the nearly 700 members associated with TCB gangs since inception on July 5th, 2017. Some people have gotten fired, arrested, and new leaders have stepped up to the plate - all in the mission of building a solid business and unique brand, with a kick ass crew.

March 2018 - A site for the brewery is negotiated, refitting of the buildings has begun, and 11 key players have grown into their roles as digital marketing specialists, project managers, brew masters and apprentices. An exclusive selection of craft beers featuring daring IPAs, smooth Lagers, and proud Stouts are under development. A marketing strategy, with logos and labels, is finalized. The grand opening of TRU Colors Brewing is slated for the bottom half of 2019.
